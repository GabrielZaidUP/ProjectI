"Potato"

by Billy Butcher

Words from the Artist:

My work explores the relationship between multiculturalism and copycat violence. With influences as diverse as Kierkegaard and Frida Kahlo, new combinations are created from both simple and complex textures. Ever since I was a pre-adolescent I have been fascinated by the endless oscillation of the universe. What starts out as contemplation soon becomes finessed into a cacophony of lust, leaving only a sense of chaos and the dawn of a new understanding. As spatial phenomena become reconfigured through frantic and repetitive practice, the viewer is left with a testament to the darkness of our culture.
