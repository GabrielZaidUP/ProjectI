"It's me"

by Jessica Bell

Words from the Artist:

My work explores the relationship between the universality of myth and UFO sightings. With influences as diverse as Camus and Francis Bacon, new combinations are generated from both mundane and transcendant discourse. Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the theoretical limits of meaning. What starts out as vision soon becomes manipulated into a dialectic of power, leaving only a sense of chaos and the unlikelihood of a new understanding. As intermittent phenomena become frozen through frantic and critical practice, the viewer is left with a hymn to the darkness of our world.
