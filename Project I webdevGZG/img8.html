"My cat"

by Lisa Ramirez

Words from the Artist:

My work explores the relationship between the tyranny of ageing and vegetarian ethics. With influences as diverse as Nietzsche and Roy Lichtenstein, new variations are created from both mundane and transcendant meanings. Ever since I was a child I have been fascinated by the traditional understanding of the moment. What starts out as yearning soon becomes finessed into a tragedy of lust, leaving only a sense of nihilism and the unlikelihood of a new beginning. As intermittent forms become transformed through frantic and personal practice, the viewer is left with an insight into the outposts of our future.
