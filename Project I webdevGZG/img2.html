"Mr.pancake-face"

by Francis Smith

Words from the Artist:

My work explores the relationship between the universality of myth and emotional memories. With influences as diverse as Kierkegaard and Miles Davis, new insights are crafted from both orderly and random narratives. Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the essential unreality of relationships. What starts out as contemplation soon becomes corrupted into a manifesto of power, leaving only a sense of failing and the possibility of a new understanding. As momentary derivatives become reconfigured through emergent and repetitive practice, the viewer is left with a tribute to the inaccuracies of our future.
