"Little Timmy"

by Phillip Soprano

Words from the Artist:

My work explores the relationship between the Military-Industrial Complex and romance tourism. With influences as diverse as Camus and Francis Bacon, new insights are synthesised from both traditional and modern discourse. Ever since I was a teenager I have been fascinated by the essential unreality of the moment. What starts out as yearning soon becomes finessed into a tragedy of defeat, leaving only a sense of decadence and the inevitability of a new order. As subtle phenomena become frozen through studious and critical practice, the viewer is left with a clue to the limits of our existence.

Artist: Phillip Soprano
Materials: Carbon on Paper
Theme:Figures/Dreams
Art Style: Still life/Abstract Expressionism
