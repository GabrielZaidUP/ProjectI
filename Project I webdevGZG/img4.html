"The long baby man"

by Martin Wooden

Words from the Artist:

My work explores the relationship between consumerist fetishism and unwanted gifts. With influences as diverse as Machiavelli and L Ron Hubbard, new tensions are manufactured from both mundane and transcendant meanings. Ever since I was a pre-adolescent I have been fascinated by the ephemeral nature of the mind. What starts out as contemplation soon becomes debased into a tragedy of futility, leaving only a sense of failing and the unlikelihood of a new beginning. As shimmering derivatives become distorted through studious and repetitive practice, the viewer is left with an insight into the inaccuracies of our era.
